• Blood Glucose Testing

    Your doctor will set target blood sugar test results based on several factors, including:

    • Type and severity of diabetes
    • Age
    • How long you've had diabetes
    • Pregnancy status
    • The presence of diabetes complications
    • Overall health and the presence of other medical conditions

    • Meal Plans

    The right meal plan will help you improve your blood glucose, blood pressure, and cholesterol numbers and also help keep your weight on track. Whether you need to lose weight or stay where you are, a meal plan can help.

    • Exercise Plans

    When people are just beginning an exercise program, they often have three questions:

    • What kinds of exercise should I do?
    • How much should I exercise?
    • How hard should I exercise?

    Our experts can advise you what types of exercise you should do based on your medical history and a thorough medical exam.

    • Massage Therapy

    Circulation is often impaired in diabetics due to the damaging effects of elevated blood sugar levels on the cells of the body. Massage of the hands and feet can be particularly beneficial. Our massage therapists can help increase the circulation of blood and lymph, to facilitate the transport of oxygen and other nutrients into the body's tissues, including more efficiency in the uptake of insulin by cells.
